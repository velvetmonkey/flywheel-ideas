Attack on asm-5esVzf6i: geographic diversification may have turned out to be geographic distribution without true independence. That combination would have exposed that geographic diversification was still regionally clustered, because China mainland, India, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, and Vietnam are diversified countries but not diversified geopolitical theaters. A shock to one layer of that network could still propagate across the whole build plan.
