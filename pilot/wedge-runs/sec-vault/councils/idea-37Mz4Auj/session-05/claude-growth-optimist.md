Finally, I didn't surface the real counter: what if Apple's R&D productivity per dollar DECLINES? (Or competitors' rises?) Integrated stack has diminishing returns: easier to integrate when you're ahead; harder when parity approaches. I was assuming unlimited R&D runway and competitive inertia. Neither holds.
