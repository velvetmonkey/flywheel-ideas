A sanctions severance scenario or forced production relocation would shatter both near-term margin AND long-cycle R&D momentum. The assumption bundles a *structural* claim (integrated stack will keep delivering) with a *macro stability* claim (geopolitical quiescence continues) — it only takes one to fail.
