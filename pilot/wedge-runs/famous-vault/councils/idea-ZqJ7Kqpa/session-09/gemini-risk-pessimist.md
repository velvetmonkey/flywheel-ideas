This fundamental preference, when exposed to the marketplace, didn't just register as a blip; it established a new baseline for Coca-Cola's primary offering.

The market, far from rejecting the change outright as a worst-case risk analysis might have predicted, demonstrated a surprising adaptability. The inherent consumer desire for a superior flavor, as revealed in the tests, was strong enough to overcome initial brand nostalgia and the predictable vocal minority.
