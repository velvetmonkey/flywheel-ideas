Downside inescapable. Reject New Coke: company abandoned beloved icon AND failed to replace it simultaneously. Accept: profit only if blind-test preference equals sustained open-market behavior. Blind tests measure snapshot preference, not habit formation or whether consumers actually want reformulation. Company invented failure path from position of maximum strength.

Methodological flaw catastrophic. Blind tests exclude all real-world decision context: brand loyalty, emotional attachment, social signaling, competitor move.
