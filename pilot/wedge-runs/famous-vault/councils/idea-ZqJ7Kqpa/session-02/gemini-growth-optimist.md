## Initial stance (Pass 1)

We optimistically believed that superior taste would automatically translate to overwhelming market dominance. The critical error was not in the taste itself, but in the assumption that preference in a lab setting fully captures the complex, identity-driven purchasing decisions of a beloved brand's loyal fanbase. It was a bold experiment, perhaps too bold, in underestimating the power of habit and heritage.
