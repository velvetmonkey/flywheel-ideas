If this decision is assumed to have succeeded 12 months later, the clean explanation is that management identified the true causal lever: the category had become more taste-led, especially among younger full-calorie cola drinkers, and Coke finally fixed the attribute on which the challenger had momentum.

Success 12 months later would mean the controversial part of the bet was exactly the right one: the sensory upgrade was real, the consumer habit system was more elastic than critics assumed, and the research generalized.
