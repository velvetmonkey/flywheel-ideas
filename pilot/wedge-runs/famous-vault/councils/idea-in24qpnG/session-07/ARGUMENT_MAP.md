### Claim

Users may accept a subsidized device, but the long-term commitment to a constrained OS experience, lacking the breadth of applications and services they rely on, is unlikely to materialize. This bet risks not only significant financial loss but also potential brand damage, as a failed hardware venture could tarnish Amazon's reputation in future hardware endeavors and divert critical resources from more certain avenues of growth.
