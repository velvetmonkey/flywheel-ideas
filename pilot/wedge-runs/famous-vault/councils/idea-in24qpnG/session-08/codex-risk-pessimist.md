## Key risks

- The Prime subsidy may drive acquisition and experimentation without producing durable primary-device retention.
- Good-enough app coverage for shopping and media may still be insufficient once users hit missing Google services or communication workflows.

## Fragile insights

- Amazon did not need broad smartphone share; a small, high-LTV Prime niche could have been enough for the launch to count as a success.
- The 99-cent handset plus included Prime could reframe the choice from switching operating systems to intensifying an already-valued membership.
